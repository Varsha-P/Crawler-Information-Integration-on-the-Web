Product Information
Designed for humans and inspired by nature, the Samsung Galaxy S III SPH-L710 cell phone sees, listens, and responds to your requirements. A front-facing camera on this Sprint Samsung Galaxy S phone works with the Smart Stay feature to adjust the brightness for a continued viewing pleasure. Sharing content with other S III users is as simple as putting the backs of two Samsung Galaxy S III SPH-L710 cell phones together. Enjoy multitasking with the Pop up Play feature of this 16 GB Samsung Galaxy S smartphone that lets you chat with friends, watch HD videos, and surf the web simultaneously. With the Smart Alert feature, this smartphone alerts you for a missed call or text messages received. Furthermore, you can answer a call or reject it, snooze the alarm, and do lots more by simply giving out voice commands to this Samsung smartphone. Running on the Android 4.0 OS, this marble white Samsung Galaxy S III phone allows you to enjoy all applications on its 4.8-inch touchscreen.
