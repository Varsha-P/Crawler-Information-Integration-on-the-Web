A smartphone that truly performs like a pc by:
lcrpro (28 )
I got this for my wife on her birthday. She absolutely loves it! It's fast, fluid, easy to use and in my case, has better specs than a laptop I have at home! There are alot of free cool apps available on the market for this little beast. It's dual core processor allows incredible multitasking between apps. The screen resolution is amazing, very sharp and vivid images. The size of the screen is just right for the average user. Touch features are responsively accurate. Has a standard headphones input so you can hook up to your car stereo, DJ system, home theatre system, pc speakers etc. The Android OS is user friendly and generally stable, but still needs work. Battery life needs improvement, especially since its powering such a large screen and dual core processor, have to charge everyday...I know this is a usual thing with smart phones, but in this day and age we should be getting better stock batteries for power hungry phones such as these. This phone has one (1) gig of ram which is really good, so a number of apps could be opened at once without memory problems. I personally would've preferred at least two gigs because I'm a serious multitasker, but the one gig is more than sufficient for the average user. The camera captures pretty ok stills and videos, I'm a professional photographer so I don't want to be too biased...some people would be amazed at this camera's capabilities, I may tend to be like,"Ok, I guess that could work". Overall the camera gives good results for such a tiny thing. There's even an app to use the camera's led flash as a torch light without actually turning on the camera itself...proven to be very convenient. The overall performance and capabilities of this phone is so convenient and efficient that you may tend to neglect your laptop/desktop. The retail price has dropped drastically since the S3 model flooded the market, so now is a good time to purchase one of these. This is now indeed an affordable super phone! Oh, do get an Otterbox case, screen protector and charging dock when you make your purchase. Hope this review was worth your while.
