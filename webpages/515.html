Product Information
Inspired by nature, the Samsung Galaxy S III SCH-I535 cell phone sees, listens, and responds to your requirements. A front-facing camera on this Verizon Samsung Galaxy S phone works with the Smart Stay feature to adjust the brightness for a continued viewing experience. Sharing content with other S III users is as simple as putting the backs of two Samsung Galaxy S III SCH-I535 cell phones together. Enjoy multitasking with the Pop up Play feature of this 16 GB Samsung Galaxy S smartphone that lets you chat with friends, watch HD videos, and surf the web simultaneously. With the Smart Alert feature, this smartphone alerts you when there is a missed call. Furthermore, you can answer/reject a call, snooze the alarm, and do more by simply giving voice commands to this Samsung smartphone. Running on the Android 4.0 OS, this marble white Samsung Galaxy S III phone enables you to access various applications on its wide 4.8-inch HD super AMOLED touchscreen.
